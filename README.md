# Economic Impact of the 2026 FIFA World Cup

## Project Description
This project focuses on predicting the economic impact of the 2026 FIFA World Cup, which will be hosted in the United States. The World Cup is one of the largest sporting events in the world, attracting millions of visitors and generating substantial economic activity in the host country.

### Objectives:
1. **Economic Analysis**: To analyze the potential economic benefits and costs associated with the event, including tourism, infrastructure development, and job creation.
2. **Data Collection**: To gather data on previous World Cups, including visitor statistics, economic forecasts, and local business impacts.
3. **Predictive Modeling**: To develop predictive models using statistical techniques and machine learning algorithms to estimate the potential economic outcomes.
4. **Stakeholder Engagement**: To provide insights and recommendations for stakeholders, such as local governments, businesses, and community organizations.

### Outcome:
The goal is to deliver a comprehensive report that outlines the expected economic impact of the 2026 FIFA World Cup, helping stakeholders make informed decisions based on data-driven insights.

### Conclusion:
By utilizing previous World Cup data and sophisticated modeling techniques, this project aims to predict the economic repercussions of one of the largest global sporting events, ensuring that the United States is prepared to maximize the benefits of hosting the tournament.
